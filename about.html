Who Am I?

I am a Digital Strategy and Marketing professional who has recently completed an MSc in Digital Strategy and Information Systems at the University of Southampton. My work spans content, analytics, and technology ensuring every digital touchpoint is optimised for engagement, performance, and growth.

My core expertise is in Digital Channel Management, using data insights and system audits to drive measurable results. I bridge strategy and creative execution through skills in web technologies and visual design, making sure brand vision translates into real action.

As a former Managing Director, I bring hands-on experience coordinating cross-functional teams and aligning marketing, finance, and operations toward shared goals.

I have also proven strong customer-facing communication skills in a high-volume service environment. At The Wendy's Company, I earned Employee of the Month twice within five months and generated the highest volume of 5-star customer reviews in the region direct evidence that I can build genuine rapport, handle pressure, and consistently leave people with a positive experience.

I am now ready to bring this combination of strategic thinking, digital expertise, and proven communication ability into a professional marketing or digital role.
